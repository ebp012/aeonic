    SimEx was developed by Aeonic, with the majority of the coding done by Eshaan Bhargav Patel.
    SimEx, formerly Proboxels, was initially started as a mod of the game Sandboxels by R74n, before gradually improving into a fully fledged fork of Sandboxels version 1.4.

    People who did make minor contributions include Aaron D'souza, Brenden D'souza, and Pravar Gollaschlammi.
